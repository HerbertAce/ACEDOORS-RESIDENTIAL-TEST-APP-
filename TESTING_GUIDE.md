# TB3.4 One-Door Test

1. Start one-door test.
2. Complete customer and site details.
3. Add/configure one Urban door.
4. Open Quote Document and confirm it displays.
5. Open Customer Acceptance, complete all confirmations and signature, then submit.
6. Complete Customer Setup as non-account or approved account.
7. For non-account, generate the pro forma under Acceptance & Payment.
8. Review the pro forma, test Email Draft, and use Print / Save PDF.
9. Continue through payment, final measure, production, dispatch and installation.
